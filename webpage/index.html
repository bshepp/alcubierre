The Landscape
Four answers to "is the warp drive physical?"

Existence — Yes (Static Slice)

Fuchs et al. 2024 (arXiv:2405.02709) show a constant-velocity Alcubierre shift can be embedded in a Tolman-Oppenheimer-Volkoff matter shell that satisfies all four energy conditions (NEC, WEC, DEC, SEC) in the bubble interior and on the wall.

Anchor point: R₁=10 m, R₂=20 m, β=0.02 c, M = R₂c²/(6G). Reproduced independently in Warp Factory v1.0 at all four ECs = 1.0000.

Thickness — Bounded From Below

The shell cannot be arbitrarily thin. Across a 27-cell (C, R₂, β) sweep at 6 inner Δ values each, the smallest DEC-respecting thickness obeys

Δmin/R2 = κ · β / C

with κ ≈ 5 typical (median 6, mean 5.3, std 1.0; range [4.0, 6.0] for 15 bracketed cells of 27).

Form universal across the surface; numerical κ is a slice-value, not a constant. Decision-gate-B per Session 19.

Acceleration — No Useful Mechanism

ADM 4-momentum conservation forbids self-acceleration of the shell in vacuum (Schuster–Santiago–Visser 2023 Theorem 3). Three escape routes catalogued; only ordinary mass ejection is viable.

Quantitative ceiling under the most favourable Fuchs-compatible parameters (HF-Jobs sweep + Varma 2022 NR rescaling): GW recoil ≲ 0.25% of vwarp. Not enough.

Static Infrastructure — No Wall

Krasnikov-tube networks: symbolic Einstein tensor reproduces Everett–Roman 1997 Eq. 14 to 14 decimals; universal scaling ρpmin ∝ −η/ε². WEC pass-rate 0.0000 across 300 (η, ε, n) points.

Closes the static-infrastructure-network branch independently of QFT loopholes.

The Slice Composite

Within the canonical static slice — Alcubierre βxx̂ shift × spherical Fuchs-class shell × asymptotically flat vacuum exterior × steady-state metric or its Lorentz boost × 4D General Relativity — the answer is "a positive-energy bubble exists, but no classical mechanism can accelerate it to useful speeds and no static infrastructure walls can be built."

This is not the closure of the classical question. Six adjacent slices (alternate shifts, hybrid walls, time-dependent acceleration, Krasnikov-2003 QI loopholes, cosmological exterior, modified gravity) are mapped in Phase 2C and all return null; the unenumerated landscape is logged with reopening criteria as Phase 2E.

New Null Configurations Identified

At high β + low C, the geometrical cap Δ < R₂ saturates before any DEC-passing shell exists. These are null configurations — no Fuchs shell exists regardless of mass.

Adds a binding constraint to the Path-2A landscape that the analytic 2A.7 / 2A.9a derivations did not surface: high-velocity low-compactness Fuchs shells do not exist as energy-condition-positive constructions, regardless of mass.
